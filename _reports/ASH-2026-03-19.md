## Company Overview

Ashland Inc (NYSE: ASH) is a global specialty additives and ingredients company. Post its transformation from a diversified chemicals conglomerate, Ashland focuses on high-value specialty ingredients serving personal care, pharmaceuticals, coatings, construction, and energy end markets. The company is headquartered in Wilmington, Delaware, with Guillermo Novo serving as Chair and CEO. Shares outstanding total approximately 45.8 million.

## Competitive Landscape

Ashland competes with Dow (DOW), BASF, Evonik, and Lubrizol in specialty additives. Its competitive advantages include: (1) strong positions in cellulose ethers for pharmaceuticals and personal care; (2) formulation expertise that creates switching costs; (3) global manufacturing footprint. However, the specialty chemicals space is fragmented, and pricing power is moderate — particularly when raw material costs (petrochemicals, cellulose) are rising rapidly, as they are with oil at $119.

The company's focus on higher-margin specialty ingredients (post-portfolio transformation) should provide better pricing power than commodity chemicals peers. But margin compression from elevated input costs is a near-term risk that directly impacts profitability.
