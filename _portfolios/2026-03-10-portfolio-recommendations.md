The addition of FCX alongside NEM and CF creates a three-legged commodity allocation that hedges against three distinct scarcity pathways: monetary debasement (gold), food inflation (fertilizer), and industrial/AI demand against constrained mining supply (copper). The portfolio still lacks fixed income (appropriate given 60/40 failure), dedicated healthcare, and exchange operator exposure (CME/ICE), which I flag as potential deployments for the reserved cash if March FOMC or credit events create entry points.
